FarmaGest AI is committed to protecting the privacy of customers and the patients whose data is managed through the platform. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed and safeguarded when you use the FarmaGest AI platform.

1. Information Collected

Account and Business Information

When registering for FarmaGest AI, the following is collected: name, email address, pharmacy name, billing address, phone number and payment information. This information is necessary to provide and bill for the services.

Pharmacy Operational Data

The platform processes data imported or generated through normal use, including inventory records, stock movement history, financial transactions and sales data. This data remains the property of the pharmacy at all times and is processed solely to provide the services described in these Terms of Service.

Patient Health Data

FarmaGest AI processes patient records including names, contact details, prescription history and medication adherence data on behalf of the pharmacy (the data controller). FarmaGest AI acts as a data processor under GDPR Article 28. Patient data is never sold, shared with third parties for commercial purposes, or used for any purpose other than providing the platform functionality to the controlling pharmacy.

Usage Data

Certain information about how you interact with the platform is automatically collected, including log data, IP addresses, browser type, pages visited and actions taken. This information is used to improve the platform, diagnose technical issues and ensure security.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To respond to comments, questions and support requests
  • To monitor and analyse usage patterns to improve user experience
  • To detect, investigate and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on servers located within the European Union. Industry-standard security measures are implemented including AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 encryption in transit, regular security audits, and strict access controls. Only authorised personnel with a legitimate need can access data.

FarmaGest AI maintains ISO 27001-aligned information security practices and conducts penetration testing at least annually.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of the subscription plus 12 months after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted. Deletion of data may be requested at any time by contacting FarmaGest AI via the contact page. Patient health data will be deleted within 30 days of a deletion request, except where legal obligations require otherwise.

5. Sharing of Information

FarmaGest AI does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with third-party service providers assisting in operating the platform (such as cloud hosting providers and payment processors), subject to strict data processing agreements. Information may also be disclosed when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Portuguese data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, or obtain a portable copy of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact FarmaGest AI via the contact page. Responses will be provided within 30 days.

GDPR Compliance

FarmaGest AI commitment to protecting data under EU regulation.

Nota: Este documento legal está disponível apenas em inglês para garantir precisão jurídica.

Last updated: 1 January 2026

FarmaGest AI is fully committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 and the Portuguese Lei n.º 58/2019. This page describes GDPR obligations, roles, and the rights available to data subjects.

1. Roles Under GDPR

Pharmacy as Data Controller

The pharmacy using FarmaGest AI is the data controller for patient and employee personal data processed through the platform. The pharmacy determines the purposes and means of processing and is responsible for ensuring a lawful basis for that processing.

FarmaGest AI as Data Processor

FarmaGest AI acts as a data processor under GDPR Article 28, processing personal data only on documented instructions from the data controller (the pharmacy). Personal data is not processed for purposes beyond what is necessary to provide the Service.

2. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

All customers are covered by the standard Data Processing Agreement, which is incorporated into these terms by reference. The DPA governs obligations as a processor, including technical and organisational security measures, sub-processor management, and assistance with data subject requests. To request a signed copy of the DPA, contact FarmaGest AI via the contact page.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

FarmaGest AI processes personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)): processing necessary to provide the subscribed Service.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): processing for security monitoring, fraud prevention and service improvement.
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): processing required by applicable law.
  • Explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a)): where the pharmacy obtains patient consent for health data processing.

4. Data Subject Rights

Individuals whose data is processed through the platform have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access (Article 15): to obtain confirmation of and access to their personal data.
  • Right to rectification (Article 16): to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure (Article 17): to request deletion where no overriding legal basis exists.
  • Right to restriction (Article 18): to restrict how data is processed.
  • Right to data portability (Article 20): to receive data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Article 21): to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

FarmaGest AI will assist pharmacies in responding to data subject requests within the 30-day statutory timeframe. Contact FarmaGest AI via the contact page for assistance.

5. International Transfers

All personal data is processed and stored within the European Economic Area (EEA). Personal data is not transferred to third countries. Cloud infrastructure providers are certified under EU-compliant data transfer frameworks.

6. Sub-processors

FarmaGest AI uses a limited number of carefully vetted sub-processors to operate the platform, including cloud hosting, payment processing and customer support tools. All sub-processors are bound by GDPR-compliant data processing agreements. A full list of sub-processors is available on request via the contact page.

7. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach affecting customer data, affected pharmacies will be notified within 72 hours of FarmaGest AI becoming aware, in accordance with GDPR Article 33. A comprehensive incident response plan is maintained and regular security testing is conducted to minimise the risk of breaches.

8. Data Protection Officer

FarmaGest AI has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) who can be contacted via the contact page.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese supervisory authority, the Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD), at www.cnpd.pt.
