FAQ

Common questions

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. PharmAI is a month-to-month subscription and you can cancel at any time. Annual plans are refundable on a pro-rata basis for unused months.

How long does the setup take?

Most pharmacies are fully set up within one afternoon. Our onboarding wizard guides you through connecting your POS, importing existing records and configuring your preferences.

Is my patient data safe?

All patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit, hosted on EU servers and fully GDPR-compliant. You own your data at all times and can export or delete it whenever you wish.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Enterprise customers can request a custom proof-of-concept period.

The Problem We Solve

Portuguese pharmacies have been underserved for too long

Research from Universidade Lusófona revealed that 36% of pharmacy managers know they need better stock management but simply lack the time to do it properly. Meanwhile, the Ordem dos Farmacêuticos estimates that up to 50% of patients misuse prescribed medication.

These aren't just numbers. They represent lost revenue, wasted medication, and patients at risk. PharmAI was built to close this gap — making enterprise-grade pharmacy management accessible to every independent pharmacy in Portugal.

Our AI doesn't just report what happened. It tells you what's about to happen, and what you should do about it.
